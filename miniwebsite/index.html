Grease Groups

T-Birds

The T-Birds are a gang of greasers who attend Rydell High School. In the first film, they consist of Danny Zuko, Kenickie, Doody, Sonny, and Putzie.These boys typically wear black leather jackets with their logo on the back, jeans, t-shirts, and either boots or converse tennis shoes.

Scorpions

The Scorpions were local gang of bullies and degenerates, the Anti-T-Birds, lead by Leo Balmudo.Flashy cars, leather jackets, cigarettes, Marlon Brando look alikes, leather hats, and sleazy girlfriends - that's the Scorpions. Leo and Cha-Cha were the primary members.

Pink Ladies

The Pink Ladies are a group of girls consisting of Rizzo, Jan, Marty, and Frenchy. They are each dating a member of the "T" birds and routinely hang out as a large group at the Frosty Palace. At their sleepovers they smoke and drink alcohol, even though underage. Throughout the movie they can be seen sporting their Pink Ladies jackets.
