Best Of

I created this piece of work for a competition in my class. The winner’s poster had the chance to be displayed at the NAIAS car show. This piece of work holds a strong significance to me because it is one of the first piecex where I was proud of the outcome and was willingly showing others it. I put it in my portfolio to show myself that even though I didn’t win the contest, I was still proud of the work that I did. I used the bright neon color in the car to grab the audience's attention, and used pastel colors in the background to make the neon pop more.

I created this piece to try educate others about how the oceans ice is melting, causing the death of thousands of polar bears. This piece is important because if we don’t do something soon, the damage that humans have done, will be irreversible. I put this in my portfolio because I thought I was one of my more mature pieces, not just messing around.

I created this piece almost as closure for myself for this class. Throughout this year I’ve come to make really good friends in my row and although we have made a point to say that we will keep in touch, we won’t see each other five days a week. So this is like a salute to the school year and all that it gave me. It’s important to me because I included all the people I hung hung out with and I included it in my portfolio because I want everyone to see that sometimes you need to go out of your comfort zone to make friends.

171 Gallery

This poster demonstrates recycling while using dark colors to make it threatening to the eye. The text is bold to add more to make the message stand out of the poster along with working with the background. The texture of the earth and smog were made by adding shading. I made the orange stand stand out on the earth to make the message clear about what our impact has on the earth. I made the smog by using a soft brush with a very light opacity.

This is an image of a paint brush and a wacom pen intertwining with a multitude of colors in the background. I made the background by laying down bright colors and blending the seems. I the drew the wacom pen and paint brush. I made the lettering simple and clean to contrast win the design in order to stand out.

This is a poster for a animal shelter in Mount Pleasant. I made the this for my sister because she is part of the board and wanted to get more volunteers. I made it yellow and Maroon to go with the CMU colors as that is where he goes, and then had the animals circulate around that color theme. For the paw prints, I made one master square, copy, and then pasted it over the rest of the page.

102 Gallery

Four Images made only out of crabs in black and white. They form patterns or shapes, such as a scarecrow, a swingset, a house, and a rocket. The crabs were positioned to into shapes by overlapping or touching in opposing colors to carve out areas on the canvas. Also the touching of claws or body create even more changed space.

The is an image of a garden with two butterflies flying through it. It’s reflected, as if they’re falling through different dimensions. I included this image in my portfolio because it was one of the very first things we ever did on photoshop concerning pictures and I believe that I have grown a lot from this. I included this piece to showcase my growth as a student concerning my photoshop skills.

This is an image of a group of dogs scattered around in a basket of carrots. I made a point to make sure that the amount of dogs throughout the image were unbalanced so it stayed asymmetric, this is made to reflect a feeling of imbalance.
